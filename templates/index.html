    Click the arrows to begin the Envairo Node setup process


    Step 1:


    Turn up your phone screen or monitor brightness to its maximum

    Step 2:


    Turn on device and check to see that your Envario Node signal LED is flashing rapidly. If it is not flashing, flip the power switch to the off position, wait 30 seconds and flip the switch back on

    Step 3:


    Skip this step if your Envairo Node is flashing amber.

    If it is not flashing amber, click the and hold your device up to the screen, with the light facing the target box.

    Step 4:


    Enter your network information below.


    Step 5:


    Click Begin Connection and hold your device to the screen, making sure the light on the front of your Envairo Node is within the target box. Do not move your Envairo Node away from the screen until the flashing is complete.

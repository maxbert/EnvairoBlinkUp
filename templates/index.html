    Click the arrows to begin the Envairo Node setup process


    Step 1:


    Turn up your phone screen or monitor brightness to its maximum

    Step 2:


    Turn on device and check to see that your Envario Node signal LED is flashing rapidly. If it is not flashing, flip the power switch to the off position, wait 30 seconds and flip the switch back on

    Step 3:


    Skip this step If your Envario Node is flashing amber. Click the red button labeled Reset Device and hold your device up to the screen, with the two front holes facing the target box.

    Step 4:


    Enter your network information below. Make sure you enter the SSID and password correctly.


    Step 5:


    Click Begin Connection and hold your device to the screen, making sure the three dots on the front of your Envairo Node are all within the target box. Do not move your Envairo Node away from the screen until the flashing is complete.
